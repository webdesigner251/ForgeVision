Hospitality & Restaurants

Helping hotels and restaurants optimize inventory, reduce waste, and improve margins.

  • Challenges: Restaurants and hotels often struggle with inventory mismanagement, overproduction, and high food waste costs.

Solution by ForgeVision:

  • AI-Powered Waste Tracking: Uses image recognition and sensors to analyse discarded food in real time.
  • Data-Driven Menu Optimization: Helps chefs refine portion sizes and menu choices based on waste trends.
  • Cost & Profitability Insights: Provides reports on food waste reduction strategies to maximize margins.
  • Sustainability Reports: Helps businesses comply with sustainability goals and certifications

Catering & Corporate Kitchens

Data-driven insights to improve efficiency in large-scale food service operations.

  • Challenges: Large-scale food production in catering services and corporate cafeterias faces inconsistent demand, excess food waste, and operational inefficiencies.

Solution by ForgeVision:

  • Real-Time Monitoring: AI sensors track food waste, providing instant feedback for chefs and managers.
  • Data-Backed Portion Control: Helps adjust portion sizes and inventory based on consumption patterns.
  • Predictive Analytics: Forecasts food demand to prevent overproduction.
  • Seamless Integration: Connects with existing POS (Point of Sale) and inventory management systems.

Healthcare & Education

Smart tracking solutions for hospitals and schools to minimize food waste and enhance sustainability.

  • Challenges: Hospitals, schools, and universities produce high food waste due to unpredictable consumption and strict dietary regulations.

Solution by ForgeVision:

  • AI-Driven Food Waste Audits: Tracks food disposal across different meal times and patient/student preferences.
  • Custom Reports for Dietary Compliance: Ensures hospitals and schools meet nutritional and portion guidelines while reducing waste.
  • Operational Efficiency Tools: Helps food service teams improve meal planning and portion control.
  • Sustainability & Cost Savings: Reduces food waste, lowering costs and environmental impact.
